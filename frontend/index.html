Everything you need to know about filing a complaint and using Civic Saathi.

What is Civic Saathi?+
Civic Saathi is a smart governance platform that connects citizens with government departments. Residents can report local civic problems like potholes, broken streetlights, water leakage, or fire hazards and track their resolution.
How do I file a complaint?+
Register an account, then go to the Report Issue page. Upload a photo, select the issue type, enter your location, and add a brief description. Our system automatically routes it to the correct department.
Why do I need to register first?+
Registration ensures authenticity and prevents spam complaints. It also allows you to track the progress of your complaints and receive updates on their resolution.
Which departments handle which issues?+
Municipal handles roads and garbage. Electricity handles outages and poles. Water Supply handles leaks and pipelines. Fire Department handles emergencies and hazards.
How long does it take to get a response?+
Your complaint is routed to the relevant department within minutes of submission. Resolution times depend on the department and nature of the issue.
Which cities does it cover?+
Civic Saathi is available across India — Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Jabalpur, and many more. The more citizens who join, the more effective the platform becomes.
