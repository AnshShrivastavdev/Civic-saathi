# CIVIC_SATHI

📌OVERVIEW <br>
Civic Saathi is a web-based platform designed to simplify the process of reporting and resolving public issues such as potholes, garbage accumulation, broken streetlights, water leakage, and other civic problems.
The platform allows citizens to easily report issues by uploading images and selecting the relevant authority responsible for addressing the issue. These reports are verified using AI-powered image analysis and then forwarded to the appropriate authorities for action.
Our goal is to bridge the gap between citizens and authorities by creating a transparent, efficient, and easy-to-use grievance reporting system.
<br>
🚀PROBLEM STATEMENT<br>
Many civic problems remain unresolved because:

Citizens often do not know where or how to report problems

Existing complaint systems are slow or complicated

Fake or irrelevant complaints waste administrative resources

There is limited transparency and tracking of complaints

Civic Saathi aims to solve these issues by providing a simple and intelligent reporting platform.

<br>
💡SOLUTION<br>
Civic Saathi provides a centralized platform where citizens can:

Register and log in

Upload an image of the issue

Provide a short description and location

Submit the complaint easily

The system uses AI-based image verification to check whether the uploaded image actually represents the reported issue. This helps reduce false complaints and ensures that authorities receive genuine reports.
<br>

🧠 KEY FEATURES<br>
📷 Image-based complaint submission

🤖 AI-powered verification of reported issues

🗂 Centralized complaint management system

👤 User registration and login

📊 Transparent grievance reporting and tracking
<br>

🌍IMPACT<br>
Civic Saathi aims to improve the public grievance system by making it easier for citizens to report problems and helping authorities respond more efficiently. The platform encourages active citizen participation and promotes better governance through technology.
